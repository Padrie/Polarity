# Polarity

![Header](https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzIwMjE1MzI4LnBuZw==/original/leuAwS.png)

- Polarity is a 2D Platformer in a steampunk environment and difficult level design. Make your way through the levels while changing the gravity of your player in different direction, use this to your advantage to overcome difficult obstacles and progressing through increasingly more difficult passages.

<h2 align="left">Features</h2>

- Change the Gravity of your player
- Overcome many special obstacles
- Difficult level design
- three different areas to overcome
- and much more...
